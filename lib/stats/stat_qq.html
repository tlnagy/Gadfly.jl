Stat.qq

Generates quantile-quantile plots for x and y. If each is a numeric vector, their sample quantiles will be compared. If one is a Distribution, then its theoretical quantiles will be compared with the sample quantiles of the other.

Aesthetics

  • x: Data or Distribution to be plotted on the x-axis.

  • y: Data or Distribution to be plotted on the y-axis.

Examples

plot(x=rand(Normal(), 100), y=rand(Normal(), 100), Stat.qq, Geom.point)
plot(x=rand(Normal(), 100), y=Normal(), Stat.qq, Geom.point)
